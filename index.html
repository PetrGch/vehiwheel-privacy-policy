Privacy Policy for VehiWheel

Effective Date: April 2, 2025

VehiWheel (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides free driving education tools for users preparing for the New Jersey MVC knowledge test. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application "VehiWheel".

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited data necessary to improve the performance and usability of the App.

a. Non-Personal Information

  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage statistics
  • Anonymous crash reports
  • In-app interaction metrics

Collected via:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

b. No Personal Identifiable Information (PII)

We do not collect your name, email, address, phone number, or any form of personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use the Data

  • Monitor app performance
  • Improve features and content
  • Detect and fix crashes
  • Understand which educational content is most helpful

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal information.

We use third-party services solely for analytics and stability monitoring:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

4. Children’s Privacy

VehiWheel is intended for users aged 16 and older (with parental guidance if younger). We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13.

5. Data Retention

We retain anonymized app usage data for as long as necessary to analyze app performance and user engagement. You may delete the app at any time to stop data collection.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no data for you to request, change, or delete. However, if you have concerns, feel free to contact us.
